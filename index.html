the need
Arteponse challenges the idea of a typical walk in and look at art when visiting gallery spaces. We want visitors to have a great and memorable and almost spiritual experience when visiting galleries.
the app

Arteponse is an innovative mobile app that allows you to have an absolute different experience when visiting art galleries. Arteponse transforms static art images to stunning slow motion shots. You are invited to listen and read the inspiration and the story behind the artwork, ultimately giving you enough knowledge and bases to fairly judge the art. The mobile app allows you to give a creative response to on an art piece like never before. Your art gallery experience will be never be the same again! An interactive, fun, and intimate visit awaits you with Arteponse.

how it works

CAPTURE
Make static images move and smile

Capture static art pieces with your mobile camera and watch it come alive with Arteponse. Using Parallax 2.5D effect to make images move and smile, giving you a different experience when looking at art. By making static images move we hope you can have a unique connection and let you experience a different way of looking at pieces from the past, present, and the future.

LISTEN
Listen to the artist tell you a story

Read and listen as the artist describes the inspiration and story behind the art piece. Giving you more knowledge about the piece and allowing you to have an intimate connection to it. Stories and art walk hand-in-hand. Don’t miss the back story of every beauty anymore.

FEEL
Share your thoughts and feelings

Increase your interaction with the art in a different and more personalized way. Arteponse allows you to be more engaged with the art itself by giving you the ability to express your opinion on the piece. You can release your thoughts by giving feedback or reacting to the art, and you can even come up with a creative response by drawing on a canvas on the app. This will allow the audience to have a more interactive relationship with the art and expressing their feelings and thoughts as they observe, analyze, and feel the artwork they are looking at.

EXPLORE
Find out what other people feel around the space

Using the data we extract from the publics reactions and expressions the app creates a map of emotions. The app documents the collective mood of the art piece and allow people to consider the unique factors that influence how we feel. It also allows the gallery to have an idea of how their visitors feel about certain pieces. It also lets the user leave their mark on the gallery by voicing their opinion and emotion on the gallery’s emotion map.
